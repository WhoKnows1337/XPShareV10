# XP-Share Project Overview

## Purpose
XP-Share is a platform for sharing paranormal, extraordinary, and unusual experiences with AI-powered pattern discovery and community insights. Users can submit experiences via text, audio, photos, or sketches, and the system uses AI to categorize, analyze, and find patterns across submissions.

## Key Categories
1. **UFO** - Unidentified flying objects and aerial phenomena
2. **Paranormal** - Ghost encounters, supernatural experiences
3. **Dreams** - Prophetic or unusual dreams
4. **Synchronicity** - Meaningful coincidences and patterns
5. **Psychedelic** - Altered consciousness experiences
6. **NDE** - Near-death experiences
7. **Meditation** - Spiritual experiences during meditation
8. **Astral Projection** - Out-of-body experiences
9. **Time Anomaly** - Time distortions and temporal experiences
10. **Entity** - Contact with non-human entities
11. **Energy** - Energy sensing and manipulation
12. **Other** - Miscellaneous extraordinary experiences

## Core Innovation
- **12 "Aha Moments"**: AI-powered pattern detection including:
  - Vector similarity search (find similar experiences)
  - Geographic clustering (wave detection)
  - Temporal patterns (time-based correlations)
  - External event correlation (solar storms, moon phases)
  - Cross-category insights
